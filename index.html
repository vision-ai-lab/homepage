Vision AI Laboratory All you can see, All you can AI

At Vision AI Lab, we specialize in advanced AI research in computer vision and imaging, focusing on real-world industrial applications.

Explore Research → Join the Lab
Research Focus

AI that understands visual, industrial, and document data.

Our lab aims to solve all the problems that humans visually perceive and recognize in videos and documents. In particular, we focus more on project-oriented research to develop AI products that can be practically used in the industry.

Research detail →
VLM · Tracking · Re-ID · Scene Understanding

Computer Vision AI

Multi-modal vision-language models, object tracking, key-frame selection, and image/video retrieval for real-world visual intelligence.
Restoration · Enhancement · Low-quality Vision

Imaging AI

Enhancement algorithms for low-quality, low-light, and noisy images across visible, IR, thermal, and X-ray imaging domains.
Industrial AI · X-ray · CT · Manufacturing

Defect Detection & Classification

AI-driven automated inspection systems for semiconductor, EV battery, food, and high-speed manufacturing lines.
OCR · LLM · Privacy · Security Classification

Document AI

LLM and OCR-based intelligent document processing for automation, privacy protection, semantic extraction, and classification.
